# AI Music Generation 2025

## The Two Giants

### Suno
- Founded in Cambridge, MA
- v4.5 model generates 90+ second complete songs in under 60 seconds
- "Personas" feature remembers user style preferences
- Strength: End-to-end song generation, fast

### Udio
- Former Google DeepMind team
- Transformer + autoregressive model
- Strength: Complex instrumental arrangements, more control

**Summary**: Suno for quick and simple, Udio for fine-grained control.

---

## Open Source Alternatives

### YuE (HuggingFace)
- Lyrics to song
- Up to 5 minutes long
- Multilingual, multi-genre
- Developer: Ruibin Yuan

### Phonira
- Based on MusicGen
- Post-processing and extending AI audio
